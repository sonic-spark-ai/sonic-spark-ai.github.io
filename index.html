AI based On-Demand Service Platform

Helpy

Helpy is an innovative on-demand service platform where service providers can offer any service—from babysitting to grocery shopping, home cleaning, and beyond. The entire process is managed through an advanced AI-powered chatbot, allowing providers to easily register and set up their offerings while enabling consumers to effortlessly find and request the services they need. By leveraging state-of-the-art LLM technology, Helpy provides a seamless, intelligent, and user-friendly way to connect service providers and consumers in real time

AI Guided Learning platform

Study Sprint

Study Sprint is an AI-based guided learning platform designed to help users evaluate and enhance their skills and knowledge through intelligent quizzes. Users can create subjects by uploading documents, books, or images of book pages, and the AI will automatically generate personalized learning paths and questions tailored to the uploaded content. This allows learners to efficiently grasp new topics and track their progress, making Study Sprint an ideal tool for structured self-study and academic growth.
